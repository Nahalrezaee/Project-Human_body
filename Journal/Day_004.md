# 📅 Journal — Day 4

Today, I continued my exploration of the human heart by focusing on how blood moves through its different parts.

After understanding the role of heart valves, the next step was to understand the complete pathway of blood flow and how each chamber and valve works together to maintain continuous circulation.

Instead of looking at the heart as a single organ, I started viewing it as a coordinated system where every part has a specific role. Understanding these connections is important before moving into more complex topics about the heart and the circulatory system.

This stage of the project is helping me build a stronger foundation by connecting individual concepts together rather than studying them separately.
