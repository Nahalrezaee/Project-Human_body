## Day 2 — Beginning with the Heart

Today I started the first topic of Project Human: the heart.

Before looking for detailed explanations, I spent time thinking about the questions that naturally came to my mind. I realized that these questions would guide my learning better than simply following a list of facts.

I explored different learning resources to decide which ones would become the foundation of this project. After comparing several options, I chose Khan Academy as my primary learning resource and an illustrated human body book as my main visual reference.

Rather than trying to answer every question today, I focused on defining the direction of my study. Understanding the heart will take time, and today's work marks the beginning of that process.
