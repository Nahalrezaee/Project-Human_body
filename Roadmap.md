# Project Human Roadmap

This roadmap defines the structure and development stages of Project Human.

The project focuses on understanding the human body through biology, programming, and mathematical thinking, and is designed as a step-by-step learning and exploration system.

---

## Phase 1: Biological Foundations
- Understand the basic structure of human body systems
- Focus on the cardiovascular system (heart and blood circulation)
- Learn core biological concepts required for deeper analysis

---

## Phase 2: Analytical Understanding
- Study how different body systems function and interact
- Move from memorization to conceptual understanding
- Start asking scientific “why” and “how” questions

---

## Phase 3: Computational Exploration
- Introduce Python for simple scientific modeling
- Represent biological processes using basic simulations
- Develop logical and structured thinking about systems

---

## Phase 4: Integrated Scientific Projects
- Combine biology and programming into small research-based projects
- Document findings in a structured scientific format
- Refine ability to explain complex systems clearly

---

## Final Goal of the Project
To build a structured understanding of the human body through continuous learning, exploration, and computational thinking.
