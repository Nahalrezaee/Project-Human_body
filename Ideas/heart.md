# Heart

## 🟡 Questions:

### What is the heart?

When I started learning about the heart, I noticed that almost every explanation immediately described what it does. Before understanding its function, I wanted to understand what the heart actually is from a biological perspective.

---

### How does the heart pump blood?

People often say that the heart "pumps blood," but I realized I had never really thought about how an organ can continuously move blood throughout the body. That curiosity led me to this question.

---

### What happens to the body if the heart stops beating for a few seconds?

This question came to my mind while thinking about how dependent the human body is on the heart. I became curious about how quickly other organs, especially the brain, respond when blood circulation is interrupted, even for a very short time.
